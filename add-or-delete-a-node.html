Based on your business requirement, anytime in the workflow, you can either add a new node in between the existing nodes or even choose to remove the node that appears next in line. 

While removing a node, the system deletes the rest of the workflow definition that lies after that node. Once deleted, all the underlying definition is permanently lost and cannot be recovered again.

   
In case of a multi-branch split applied, you can delete the individual nodes
that lie within the split. But, when you try to delete a multi-branch split,
both the underlying individual nodes are deleted as well.
